Request an authentication code to be sent to a home address

Use this service to get a company's authentication code posted to an active director's home address instead of the registered office.

You'll need:

  • the company number
  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account, which is different from a WebFiling account

You cannot use this service if:

  • the company is not based in the UK
  • the company is not a limited company or limited liability partnership (LLP)
  • the company is dissolved

How long it takes

It takes around 5 working days for a director to receive an authentication code at their home address.

Due to the impact of coronavirus (COVID-19), it may take longer than usual for post to be delivered.

! Warning You should only use this service if you know the director has given us their most recent home address. For security reasons, you cannot see or update the address in this service.
